  • Help4TheHomeless is a nonprofit organization in which people in the community help out the homeless. When you are homeless, a simple smile and a word of kindness can make a big difference in a day full of hardship. Our website helps the homeless by donating or volunteering to the campaign in giving out money, clothing, and shoes, etc. to the homeless and provides services that meet their immediate needs and transforms their lives from poverty and hopelessness to hope.

    • *Our purpose

    The purpose of Help4TheHomless is to minister the Homeless caught in the cycle of poverty,hopelessness and homless and dependecies of many kind and to see their lives transform to joy, lasting productivity and eternal life.

    To be the most effective provider of compassionate care and life tramsformation for homless people in The US.
